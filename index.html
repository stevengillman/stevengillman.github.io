About

I am a Researcher at the Cosmic Dawn Center, National Space Institute (DTU-Space), Denmark. My research focuses on the formation and evolution of galaxies. I am part of the European MIRI GTO team working on the MIRI Deep Imaging Survey (MIDIS).
